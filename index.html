UWL Site naming conventions

When a location in UWL is described to you, the naming convention would be something like the example below



BY.03.001

In this example, BY is the name of the section that the room is located in(Lady Byron)

The 03 refers to the floor number (03), or the third floor

The 001 refers to the number of the room in that section
